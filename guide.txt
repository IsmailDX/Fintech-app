--------------------------------------------------
‚ôªÔ∏è WHEN TO RE-RUN PREBUILD --clean
--------------------------------------------------
Run this command:

- You change babel.config.js
- You change app.json / app.config.js
- Video/audio/camera libs stop working
- App behaves differently across machines
